Top 5 Streamers from League of Legends

On this page I will comment 5 of the biggest League of Legends streamers in the world.

Tyler1


Tyler1 is American, his Twitch channel has the most followers among League of Legends streamers, and among League of Legends channels in general he only loses to Riot's own channel, the game's creator, there was a time where he was banned from LoL for his behavior with other players, and thus earned the nickname "The Most Toxic Player in North America". In October 2020, Tyler joined the South Korean esports team T1 as a content creator. His Twitch channel has more than 3.7 million followers, with more than 7.5 million hours watched in his lives in October alone (2020), and has almost 2.5 million subscribers on YouTube.

Faker


Faker was born in South Korea, he is a professional League of Legends player, he can even be considered the best, he has been playing for the T1 team for a long time, he has won the League of Legends World Championship 3 times, and he has passed the US $ 1,000,000 in cash prizes. Currently (October, 2020) his Twitch channel has almost 3 million followers, and in October 2020 he had almost 1.4 million hours watched in his streams, Faker also has a Youtube channel, having the same content, gameplays League of Legends, your channel already has more than 1.2 million subscribers.

YoDa


YoDa is a Brazilian who has played League of Legends professionally, has worked in teams such as RED Canids, Fit4Fight, LegendsBR, IMP e-Sports, CNB e-Sports Club, IDM Gaming and Submarino Stars. YoDa also founded the SehLoiro Network, the YoTalkShow program and a clothing and accessories brand for gamers. It is the biggest Brazilian streamer in League of Legends, with over 1 million subscribers on his YouTube channel and almost 1.9 million followers on his Twitch channel, and still has almost 3.5 million hours watched in his streams during the month October 2020.

Sneaky


Sneaky is a professional League of Legends player, he played for Cloud9 until 2019, in addition to playing he is famous for cosplaying female League of Legends characters, in 2020 he left the competitive scene a little aside to focus more on his streams. His Twitch channel has already passed 1.6 million followers, and in October 2020 had almost 1.4 million hours watched on his streams, he also has a YouTube channel, with more than 470 thousand subscribers.

Jukes


Jukes is another well-known Brazilian League of Legends streamer, he has participated in some teams, such as Submarino Stars and Cloud9, he was famous for his advanced gameplays and rages in streams. Currently he has almost 1.2 million subscribers on YouTube almost 1.4 million followers on Twitch, and in October 2020 he got more than 1 million hours watched in his lives.
