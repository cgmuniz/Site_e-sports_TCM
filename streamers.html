Great Streamers

It is possible to notice that there are several successful streamers out there, and on this page I will tell you some cases of streamers that got along very well and are now famous and making a lot of money from their streams.

Ninja


You may already know Ninja, or at least you've heard of him, he was born in the United States, and burst onto the internet in 2017 playing Fortnite, and today he is one of the biggest streamers in the world, even getting his own skin inside Fortnite. He started with streams in 2011, and during the journey until his burst on the internet he broadcast games like H1Z1, PUBG and Halo, after he started playing fortnite in his streams he grew a lot, following the growth and fame of the game, he already broke records of simultaneous audience on Twitch (more than 600 thousand simultaneous people). Currently (October 2020) he has more than 24 million subscribers on YouTube and more than 16 million followers on Twitch, and in 2019 he was the streamer who most profited worldwide, earning more than $ 17 million.

Tfue


Tfue was born in the United States, and it's another case of a streamer who grew up with Fortnite, he became famous mainly because of this game, became a great player, and ended up even joining a great professional esports organization, the FaZe Clan. In 2019 he sued the organization for alleged abuse of the contract, and now he apparently is no longer part of the organization, currently he does not dedicate 100% just to Fortnite and plays other games in streams too. Tfue has more than 12 million subscribers on YouTube and more than 9 million followers on Twitch.

Shroud


Shroud was born in Canada, he was a professional CS:GO player, he became part of Cloud 9, but he left the game in 2017. In 2017 PUBG was released, so he took advantage and started playing it in his streams, from then on he has already started to grow on Twitch and continued to follow the releases of FPS and Battle Royales, even playing games like Fortnite, Apex Legends and Valorant. Shroud currently has more than 6.5 million subscribers on YouTube and more than 8 million followers on Twitch.

Pokimane


Pokimane is one of the biggest streamers in the world, was born in Morocco, and gained fame for her live League of Legends and Fortnite gameplays, but has recently been playing other games as well. She also has a YouTube channel, where she has almost 6 million subscribers, and posts videos of various things, as well as gameplays for various games. Streams of different themes and being the biggest female streamer on Twitch, and she has more than 6 million followers on the platform.

Alanzoka


Alanzoka is the greatest Brazilian streamer, he plays several games in his streams, and whatever game he is broadcasting, he always gets an incredible amount of viewers, he also excelled in games like Apex Legends, Fortnite and Overwatch, and despite playing several games from different categories in his lives, his YouTube channel ends up having a focus on horror games, he has more than 6.5 million subscribers on YouTube and almost 4 million followers on Twitch.
