Top 3 Streamers from Valorant

On this page I will comment 3 of the biggest Valorant streamers in the world.

Ninja


You may already know Ninja, or at least you've heard of him, ninja was born in the United States, and burst onto the internet in 2017 playing Fortnite, and today he is the streamer with the most followers in the world. He recently is not playing Fortnite as much as before, he has also been broadcasting Valorant streams, he was already a giant streamer before Valorant, carrying the 1st position of greatest streamer in the world, so he ends up also being the greatest streamer of Valorant, and as he is best known for Fortnite he doesn't get as many viewers on Valorant as he did before, but he still remains a great streamer. Currently (October 2020) he has more than 24 million subscribers on YouTube and over 16 million followers on Twitch, and in October 2020 he got over 2.5 million hours watched on his streams.

Hiko


Hiko was born in the United States, he was a professional CS:GO player, passing through Complexity Gaming, Cloud9 and Team Liquid teams, today he plays Valorant professionally at 100Thieves and transmits his gameplay on Twitch. Your YouTube channel has over 300,000 subscribers and your Twitch channel has over 1 million followers, in addition to having almost 1.5 million hours watched on his streams during October 2020.

Hastad


Hastad is Argentine, played League of Legends in streams, but recently started playing Valorant. During his journey, Hastad realized that the Brazilian audience that watched streams was bigger, so he decided to play in the Brazilian server and learned to speak Portuguese to win over the Brazilian audience, and it worked, went from 300 spectators to an average of 3 thousand. Its YouTube channel has more than 650 thousand subscribers and its Twitch channel more than 900 thousand followers, and in October 2020 it got more than 650 thousand hours watched in his streams.
